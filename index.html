About the Band

A Living Legacy of Swing

Jazz House Big Band brings the energy, elegance, and excitement of classic big band music to audiences throughout Eastern Idaho.

With a full ensemble of saxophones, trombones, trumpets, rhythm section, and vocalists, the band performs music inspired by the great sounds of Count Basie, Duke Ellington, Glenn Miller, Benny Goodman, and other legendary artists.

From formal concerts to lively community dances, every performance is designed to preserve the spirit of the Swing Era while creating an unforgettable experience for modern audiences.
