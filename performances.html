Monthly Tradition

The First Saturday Swing Dance

A recurring evening of live music and dancing presented by Jazz House Big Band every first Saturday of the month.

Live Music.
Real Dancing.
Everyone Welcome.

Whether you have been swing dancing for years or have never stepped onto the dance floor before, this event is for you.

Bring friends, meet new people, and enjoy the energy of a full live big band in a classic social-dance atmosphere.

Beginner Swing Dance Lessons

Arrive early and learn the basics before the dance begins! From 7:30 PM – 8:00 PM, we offer a $2 beginner lesson taught in a relaxed, welcoming environment.

No experience is necessary, no partner is required, and everyone is encouraged to join.
